# Conversion Engine

Diagnose before rewriting.

## Mandatory Upstream Gate

Before every recommendation or rewrite, ask:

> **Are we trying to solve a problem that belongs to another layer?**

Classify every finding into one primary layer:

| Layer | Name | Question |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Business / Positioning | Who is this for, what transformation is sold, and does the homepage match it? |
| 2 | Messaging | Is the message communicating the positioning, or compensating for unclear positioning? |
| 3 | Offer | Is the offer weak, or is positioning making a good offer look weak? |
| 4 | Trust | Is trust missing, or is proof compensating for weak messaging? |
| 5 | Conversion | After Layers 1-4 pass, what blocks action: CTA, layout, copy, forms, length, mobile, speed, or flow? |

Work from Layer 1 downward. Record downstream issues, but mark them `deferred`
when an upstream decision controls the outcome.

## Inputs

Use the available URL, pasted copy, screenshots, offer, audience, goal, traffic
context, and proof. State what is missing.

Do not infer final business positioning from the website alone. When audience,
transformation, category, or differentiation evidence is missing, return
`business decision required`.

## Recommendation Contract

For every recommendation include:

- observed symptom
- primary layer
- upstream-layer test
- root cause and evidence
- recommendation
- status: `act now`, `business decision required`, or `deferred`
- rewrite eligibility

## Evidence Contract

For every finding include:

- page, section, or source
- exact quote, screenshot observation, or behavior
- visitor impact
- root cause
- exact fix
- replacement copy or structure when relevant

Do not score categories that were not observed. Do not invent conversion rates,
testimonials, urgency, or results.

## Workflow

1. Run the five-layer free audit.
2. Identify the highest unresolved upstream layer.
3. Build the roadmap in layer order.
4. Recommend paid rewrite only when Layer 1 is sufficiently clear.
5. Before paid rewrite, create a pre-write plan covering audience, outcome,
   offer, mechanism, proof, objections, CTA, funnel path, and page map.
6. Recommend a service call when the site requires redesign or rebuilding.

## Product Boundary

- Free: diagnosis, five-layer scorecard, priorities, and roadmap.
- Paid: complete section-by-section copy rewrite.
- Service: website redesign or rebuild after a call and scoped engagement.
- Tool: separate product in progress, not part of this skill release.

Do not expose a self-serve website builder or public HTML rebuild.

## Output

Return:

- evidence mode and inputs
- five layer scores supported by evidence
- primary root layer
- top findings with layer classification
- exact diagnosis and fix brief
- what to keep
- what to defer
- paid rewrite eligibility
- rebuild service recommendation
- seven-day roadmap
- one next step

## Boundaries

- Route SEO, GEO, AEO, crawlability, schema, and AI citations to
  `visibility-audit`.
- Route combined audits to `website-audit`.
- Never rewrite directly from a vague request or unsupported assumptions.
- Never solve unclear positioning with better-sounding copy.
- Never provide a complete free rewrite inside the audit.
